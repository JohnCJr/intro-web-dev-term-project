Events for the month of November 2022

Field Day

Field day is right around the corner. What is field day, you ask? Field day is an event held in place of regularly scheduled classes where students compete in a variety of events. Students will compete against other classes in their grade level for points based on the events. The events are still to be decided. Students are to report to their classroom as usual at the start of the day. Mark your calendars for October 3, 2022.


Make sure to check your local weather forecast the day before the event. Field day will be postponed in the event of server weather conditions

Parent-Teacher Conference

Parents, here your chance to have a one-on-one with your child's teacher! See where your children are excelling and where they can make improvements. Feel free to ask about what you can do to facilitate their academic progress. The conference begins on October 28, 2022 at 5:00 PM and are about 15 minutes sessions.

Book Buddies

Students of all ages are welcome to attend this event being held at the West Pine Public Library after school. This is an even that encourages students to find others that share a passion for reading and make new friends. This event will be held on November 18, 2022 at 3:30 PM. Parents are also welcome to attend.

Recycling Assembly

An assembly will be held to teach students about all the ways that they can do their part to keep the planet clean for years to come November 29, 2022 at 2:30 PM.


To learn more about what can be recycled, check out the National Institute of Environmental Health Sciences (NIEHS) Kids page.
